Hi I'm Łukasz Szymański

I'm a self-taught enthusiast of coding, classical music, and video production.
This space reflects my creative journey where I merge the worlds of technology, music, and visual storytelling.

I am a musician with many years of stage experience and a teacher. During my musical career, I had the pleasure of performing on many Polish and European stages with outstanding musicians and conductors. Since 2018, I have been an active participant of the "Classical Music Festival" in Eisenstadt. In 2023, I had the pleasure of working with outstanding musicians from the chamber orchestra "The Splitzki Virtuozi", where we performed several concerts together, including: Split, Zadar, Durbovnik. Beyond my musical endeavors, I am deeply involved in educational pursuits, fostering a nurturing environment where I impart my wealth of knowledge, offer valuable insights, and instill a profound love for music. Through my teaching activities, I aim to empower aspiring musicians, guiding them through their artistic journey, and igniting their creative spark.

I entered the world of programming as an autodidact, starting with a short course in cybersecurity. Even though my first steps were directed towards the frontend, my enthusiasm for learning pushed me to expand my horizons during the first two years of my IT adventure. As a front-end developer, I can boast of participating in several fascinating projects, including creating websites for a neurologist and a pediatrician. Each of these projects was an opportunity not only to use my technical skills, but also to create solutions that had a significant impact on people's lives. On March 17, 2024, I joined the prestigious "Za Rączka" mentoring program, where I started my adventure with backend development and other fascinating aspects of the IT world. This new path opens up unlimited possibilities for me, giving me the opportunity to constantly develop and explore new areas of technology.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me behind the lens of a camera. Video creation is my way of sharing stories and capturing emotions in cinematic frames. This passion drives me to constantly improve my craft, from mastering editing techniques to experimenting with different filming styles. I enjoy the collaborative nature of video production, working with directors, sound designers, and actors to achieve shared artistic goals. My portfolio includes various formats - from short films and documentaries to music videos and commercial content, keeping my work dynamic and engaging.
